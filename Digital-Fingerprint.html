360-DEGREE CUSTOMER VIEW

Salesforce Customer 360 is a powerful tool and strategy provided by Salesforce to help organizations create a unified and comprehensive view of their customers across different touchpoints and systems. It enables businesses to break down data silos and connect customer data from various sources into a single, centralized platform, allowing for a holistic understanding of each customer's interactions, preferences, and behaviors.

Key components and benefits of Salesforce Customer 360:

  • Data Integration
  • Single Customer View
  • Personalized Customer Experiences
  • Data Privacy and Consent Management
  • Customer Service Efficiency
  • Data Governance and Security
  • Scalability and Flexibility
